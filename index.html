How to play
Objective:
  • Push the ball into the opponent goal
Controls:
  • Use your mouse cursor to control direction
  • Click mouse button to BOOST
  • Hold SPACE to BRAKE / STAND STILL
  • Press T to chat. Press G then any 0-9 key for quickchat
  • Press TAB to display player statistics
  • Gamepad connected !
  • Use your gamepad stick to move
  • Click button 1 to BOOST
  • Release stick or click button 2 to BRAKE / STAND STILL
  • Press T to chat
Camera:
  • Press C to switch between fullscreen or follow-player
  • In follow-player mode, press X to switch between
    • (1) centered on player
    • (2) centered on player and field
    • (3) fixed zoom (use mouse wheel to zoom in/out)
